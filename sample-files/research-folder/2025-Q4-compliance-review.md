# Compliance Review — Rewards Program Restructuring
**Date:** November 14, 2025
**Attendees:** Rachel Foster (Chief Compliance Officer), Sarah Chen (VP Product), David Kim (CTO), Amir Hassan (Legal Counsel)

## Purpose
Review the proposed 3-tier rewards restructuring for regulatory and compliance implications before development begins.

## Issues Identified

### Issue 1: Tier Migration Notification (RESOLVED)
**Regulation:** FCAC Guidelines on Disclosure of Charges
**Concern:** Moving customers between tiers constitutes a material change to their account terms. Customers must receive 30 days written notice before any change takes effect.
**Resolution:** Sarah confirmed the communication plan includes 45-day advance notice via email + in-app notification + mailed letter for customers without email on file. Rachel approved this approach.
**Status:** ✅ Resolved

### Issue 2: Points Conversion Fairness (PENDING)
**Regulation:** Consumer Protection Framework — Fair Treatment of Customers
**Concern:** Converting existing points from the 5-tier system to the new 3-tier system must not result in any customer receiving less value than they currently hold. The proposed conversion formula needs legal review to confirm no customer is disadvantaged.
**Detail:** Amir identified an edge case where customers currently in the "Silver" tier (being eliminated) could lose access to Silver-specific redemption options before they've had a chance to use accumulated points. The proposed 90-day grace period may not be sufficient for customers with large point balances.
**Action required:** Legal to model worst-case scenarios for Silver-tier customers with >50,000 points and propose a fair conversion mechanism.
**Status:** ⏳ Pending legal review — target completion December 15, 2025
