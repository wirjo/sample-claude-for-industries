| Estimated annual revenue loss from rewards churn | $4.4M |
| Cost to acquire replacement customer | $340 (vs. $45/year retention cost) |

## Recommendation
The data strongly supports the rewards restructuring initiative. Customer frustration is accelerating, not stabilizing. Every quarter we delay costs approximately $1.1M in preventable churn. The restructuring should prioritize simplicity and transparency above all else — customers aren't asking for more rewards, they're asking to understand the ones they have.
